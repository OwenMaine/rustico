Our Services

Custom Barn Doors

Handcrafted sliding barn doors customized to your specifications. Available in various styles including classic, modern, rustic, and contemporary designs. Each door is made with premium materials and includes professional hardware installation.

Rustic Furniture

Custom-built rustic furniture pieces including dining tables, coffee tables, benches, and storage solutions. Each piece is uniquely designed to match your space and style preferences, crafted from high-quality wood.

Bifold Doors

Space-saving bifold door solutions perfect for closets, room dividers, and pantries. Custom-sized and styled to your needs, with smooth-operating hardware and professional installation included.

Door Installation

Professional installation services for all door types. Our expert team handles everything from hardware mounting to track installation, ensuring perfect operation and finish. Includes interior, exterior, and sliding door installations.

Door Repair & Maintenance

Comprehensive door repair and maintenance services including hardware replacement, track adjustment, panel repair, and refinishing. We service all types of doors including sliding, bifold, and traditional swing doors.

Custom Exterior Doors

Durable and stylish exterior doors designed to withstand the elements while maintaining beauty. Available in various styles and finishes, with weather-stripping and proper insulation for energy efficiency.

Trim Work

Expert trim work and finishing touches that enhance the beauty of your doors and spaces. Including custom moldings, casings, baseboards, and decorative trim elements to complete your project.

Pet Door Installation

Custom pet door solutions integrated seamlessly into existing doors or walls. Available in various sizes to accommodate different pets, with weather-sealed frames and secure locking mechanisms.

Custom Projects

Specialized woodworking projects tailored to your unique needs. From custom storage solutions to architectural details, we bring your vision to life with quality craftsmanship and attention to detail.
