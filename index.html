What We Do

Founded in North Billerica, MA, RUSTICROO DESIGNS LLC is a premier custom woodworking company specializing in handcrafted barn doors and rustic furniture. Our dedicated team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern functionality to create stunning pieces that enhance your living space. From custom interior and exterior doors to bespoke furniture pieces, we take pride in delivering high-quality, made-to-order products that reflect our commitment to excellence in woodworking.
